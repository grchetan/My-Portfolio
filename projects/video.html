Overview

A dynamic showcase webpage displaying my video editing work and cinematic production skills. The page highlights creative video content, transitions, color grading, and motion graphics work.

The Challenge

Creating a page that does justice to the visual quality of video content while loading quickly and not overwhelming visitors. The design needed to feel cinematic and match the energy of the video work itself.

The Solution

Designed a bold, fullscreen-first layout that lets the videos take center stage. Used autoplay previews with hover interactions to give visitors a taste of each video without requiring full playback. The dark aesthetic complements the cinematic content.

Key Features

Cinematic dark UI that complements video content
Video preview cards with hover interactions
Category filtering for different video types
Fullscreen video playback support
Responsive grid layout for all screen sizes
Fast-loading with lazy video initialization
